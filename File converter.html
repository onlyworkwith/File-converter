Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes, your files are encrypted during transfer and processing. We automatically delete free user files after 30 minutes and premium user files after 7 days. We never access the content of your files.

Are there any file size limits?

Free users can upload files up to 100MB in size. Premium users can upload files up to 500MB. For larger files, please contact our support team.

How many files can I convert at once?

Free users can convert one file at a time with a daily limit of 5 files. Premium users can convert up to 20 files simultaneously with no daily limits.

Will the conversion affect the quality of my files?

We strive to maintain the highest possible quality during conversion. However, some format conversions may result in minor quality changes due to the nature of the formats. You can choose between high quality or compressed versions for most downloads.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required for free users. Premium users need to create an account to access advanced features and manage their subscription.

Can I use this service on mobile devices?

Yes, our converter works on all modern devices including smartphones and tablets. The interface is fully responsive and optimized for touch screens.
